Facilities


To make life of Hall residents easier we have various facilities with broad categories of Guest Rooms, Common Rooms, Sports Facilities

There are six guest rooms in the hall for visitors of students to stay in. They can be booked online through this website.

The common rooms present in the hall is the reading room, TV room, HEC room. They are usually used for use by the GBM for studying/entertainment/meetings etc...

There are a variety of sports facilities available including basketball, batminton, football and volleyball ground. There is also a TT room present. The equipment necessary to play these various sports is also provided. There is also a gym present in the hall.

More information about these facilities can be found below
